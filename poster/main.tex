\myblock[MyBlock]{Introduction}{
   Color has a big influence on motion vision in zebrafish. 
   Orger and Baier (2004) used the optomotor response to show that motion blindness can be indueced to a grating of different colors in zebrafish. 
   \vspace{0.7cm}
   \begin{tikzfigure}[]
    \label{Raw}
    \includegraphics[width=17cm]{figs/omr.pdf}
    \end{tikzfigure} 
   
   But little is known about the cortical structures conveing the
   \glqq color-motion\grqq{} perception. We investigated the 
   activity of direction-selective units in the optic tectum of zebrafish in response to gratings of various color contrasts using a combination of two-photon microscopy and calcium imaging. 
}
